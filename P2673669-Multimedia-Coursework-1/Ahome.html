In the beggining it starts with two american high schoolers, one calvin "the jet" joyner and Robbie Weirdicht who is shown to be thrown naked into the gym during a speech by calvin, this bullying leads to an outburst of laughter filling the gym by everyone but calvin who instead helps him cover himself up by giving him his jacket. everyone at the school had the picture that Robbie was always going to be bullied through his life at that point allowing for him to prove them wrong. time skips forward to when they are adults and joyner is married to his old crush and is a forensic accountant, but is dissapointed with himself and his career, and his wife suggest to him they see a therapist before he sets off to work. when he is at work he recieves a facebook friend request from someone named bob stone, which he gets invited to a bar from, eventually joyner finds out robbie had transformed his life from fat to fit and has had a name change to disguise his past from bullies from his school, joyner finds out that bob stone works with the fbi and he needs his help to find the blackbadger and take them down, however it comes known to joyner that either bob stone is the blackbadger or he is being framed as him, and he is put in an uncomfortable spot between bob stone and the CIA. joyner works with bob to figure it out.
